=== ABOUT AVIETHO ===

Avietho Digital is a full‑service digital marketing agency based in Muntinlupa City, Philippines. The company was founded in 2018 by four siblings of Milarosa and George, and the name "Avietho" is derived from the first two letters of their names: AV, IE, TH, O.

Avietho empowers brands, political figures, government agencies, and businesses with innovative, data‑driven digital strategies tailored specifically for the Philippine market. The agency combines journalism, branding, public relations, multimedia production, social media marketing, and web development under one roof.

Over the years, Avietho has completed more than 100 projects and served over 50 happy clients, ranging from small local businesses to high‑profile political campaigns and government departments.

=== CORE VALUES ===

Avietho operates on four core principles:

- Strategic Excellence – every campaign is built on data and analytics, with a clear focus on measurable ROI and performance.
- Innovation – the team continuously explores the latest digital trends, tools, and platforms to keep clients ahead of the competition.
- Partnership – long‑term relationships are the foundation of the agency. Trust, transparency, and collaboration are central to every project.
- Local Expertise – deep understanding of the Philippine market, cultural nuances, and consumer behaviour ensures campaigns resonate with the target audience.
